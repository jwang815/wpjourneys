# Outreach Playbook (Cold Email + Preview Flow)

## 1) Deliverability Baseline

Before sending any campaign:

- Set SPF, DKIM, DMARC for sending domains
- Warm inboxes gradually
- Use low daily volume per inbox initially
- Verify recipient emails
- Remove role addresses when possible (`info@`, `admin@`) unless no alternative
- Process bounces and unsubscribes automatically

## 2) Personalization Framework

Every first-touch message should include:

1. Specific observation from current website
2. One concrete business downside from that issue
3. One clear improvement promise
4. Low-friction CTA

Keep under ~120 words when possible.

## 3) Sequence Timing

- Day 1: Email 1
- Day 3: Email 2
- Day 6: Email 3
- Day 10: Breakup email

Stop immediately on any negative reply or unsubscribe.

## 4) Template: Email 1 (blurred preview)

Subject: `Quick idea for {{business_name}} homepage`

Hi {{first_name_or_team}},

I was checking out {{website_url}} and noticed {{specific_observation}}.

I mocked up a cleaner homepage concept focused on {{result_goal: more calls/bookings/leads}}.
I can send the full version if useful — here is a blurred preview:
{{blurred_preview_url}}

Worth sending the full demo link?

{{sender_name}}
{{agency_name}}
Reply "no" and I will not follow up.

## 5) Template: Email 2 (value follow-up)

Subject: `Re: {{business_name}} homepage concept`

Wanted to follow up with 3 specific upgrades I'd implement:

1. Strong mobile-first CTA above the fold
2. Trust section with reviews + proof
3. Faster structure for better conversion and load time

If helpful, I can share the full interactive mockup.

{{sender_name}}
Reply "no" to opt out.

## 6) Template: Email 3 (soft close)

Subject: `Should I close this file?`

If now is not a priority, no worries — I can close this out.

If you want the full demo before deciding, just reply "send it".

{{sender_name}}
Reply "no" to opt out.

## 7) Positive Reply Handling Script

When lead replies positively:

1. Send full preview link.
2. Ask:
   - "How many pages do you need?"
   - "Any integrations (booking, CRM, payments)?"
   - "Target launch timing?"
3. Offer package options.
4. Send invoice.

## 8) Offer Packaging (example)

- Starter: 1-3 pages, fast launch
- Growth: multipage + conversion improvements
- Authority: multipage + blog/CMS + advanced integrations

Define revision limits and out-of-scope rates in writing.
